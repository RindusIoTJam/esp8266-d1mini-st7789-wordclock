# esp8266-d1mini-st7789-wordclock

## Abstract

A words based clock telling the time.

## Cabling D1 mini w/ ST7789

 D1 mini | ST7789 
 ---     | --- 
 D0      | BLK
 D1      | DC
 D2      | RES
 D3      | IR Receiver
 D5      | SCL
 D7      | SDA
 3.3V    | VCC
 G       | GND

## BOM

1. Wemos D1 mini
2. ST7789 240x240px oLED display
3. IR Receiver
